Who Am I?

Hi! My name is Kevin Sequeira. 25, Indian, Catholic.
      I'm an Acquisition Editor with Packt>, and am currently located in Mumbai, India.

I've formerly been an SAP ABAP/4 Developer at a leading multinational, but decided to drop off when things got too monotonous.
      I am a Java, Python, and R enthusiast, and make time to experiment with code at the end of my day and on holidays.

I am also Frontman of the band KAIROS, and spend a good portion of my week making music or just strumming my favorite numbers on my ASHTON D46SCEQ SEMI ELECTRO ACOUSTIC GUITAR. I love the Blues, Country, Pop-Rock, and Alt. You can find us on YouTube in this link.

I have only recently begun experimenting with Front-End Development using HTML5, CSS3 (with help from Bootstrap 4), and plan to soon dive into JavaScript. This website is my first major experiment with web development.
      With that added, I would like to highlight that I am a quick learner, and enjoy doing new stuff all the time.

I graduated as an Electronics and Telecommunications Engineer from Xavier Institute of Engineering, University of Mumbai. It was during these four years that I was introduced to programming and simulation in MATLAB. Prior to that, I'd stayed away from computers.
      During my University years I was also part of IEEE Bombay Section's Student Chapter at my college, and have served as Assistant-Chairperson and Chairperson. I have spear-headed different technical events at college, as well as organized workshops for students.

When I'm not doing any of the above I'm hanging out with my girlfriend, watching a movie alone at home, reading a book, browsing the Star Wars Wiki or LOTR Wiki, or helping out at Church.
